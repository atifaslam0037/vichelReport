Privacy & Data Protection Policy

Introduction

AUTODATA LLC ("we", "our" or "AUTODATA") is committed to protecting the privacy of all visitors, customers and clients, who access our websites, applications and services. Please read the following privacy policy which explains how we use and protect your information. By visiting, ordering and/or using our services, you agree and, where required, you consent to the collection, use and transfer of your information as set out in this policy. This privacy policy applies only to the digital properties of CARANTEE and AUTODATA (including the websites and applications) and does not extend to the websites of any other companies or organizations to which we link, or who link to us.

This privacy policy specifically addresses our obligations in respect of applicable laws in the United Arab Emirates ("UAE") and any other region that we do business in.CARANTEE is a brand name registered and owned by AutoData LLC.

Information that we collect

When you visit our Website, use our Applications and/or services or contact our customer care team, you may be asked to provide information about yourself, including your name, contact details and payment information such as credit or debit card information, as well as information about your business, including the trade name, address and contact details, as well as information about a vehicle that you inquire about, including the Vehicle Identification Number and/or any other vehicle registration number (e.g. Malkiya). We may also collect information about your usage of the Website, Application, API’s and information about you from any messages that you send to us.

We may also collect technical information from your mobile device and/or your use of our services through a mobile device, for example, location data and certain characteristics of, and performance data about your device, carrier/operating system including device and connection type, IP address, mobile payment methods, interaction with other retail technology such as use of NFC Tags, QR Codes or use of mobile vouchers. Unless you have elected to remain anonymous through your device and/or platform settings, this information may be collected and used by us automatically if you use the service through your mobile device(s) via any CARANTEE or AUTODATA mobile application, through your mobile"s browser or otherwise.

Your information will enable us to provide you with access to our websites, applications and services, and it will enable us to supply the services you have requested. It will also enable us to bill you and to contact you where necessary concerning our services. We will also use and analyze the information we collect so that we can administer, support, improve and develop our business, for any other purpose whether statistical or analytical and to help us prevent fraud.

You agree that we may use your information to let you know about our other products and services that may be of interest to you including services that may be the subject of direct marketing and we may contact you to do so by post, telephone, mobile messaging (e.g. SMS, MMS etc.) as well as by e-mail. Where you have chosen at a device or browser level to begin or continue receiving push notifications from us, we may send you push notifications relating to the services that you have requested from us and information about our services and offers.

By submitting comments and feedback regarding the Website, Application, API’s and/or the services, you consent to us to use such comments and feedback on the Website and Application, and in any marketing or advertising materials. We will only identify you for this purpose by your first name and the region in which our Website or Application was accessed and/or the service was delivered. If you do not want us to use your data in the way(s) described above, or if you change your mind about being contacted in the future, please let us know by using the contact details set out below and/or amending your profile accordingly. Where appropriate, now and in the future you may have the ability to express your preferences around the use of your data as set out in this privacy policy and this may be exercised though your chosen method of using our services, for example mobile, mobile applications or any representation of our Website.

Disclosure of your information

AUTODATA will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy. The information you provide to us will be transferred to and stored on our servers. In summary, we will only share with or disclose your information if so required by law, to deliver the services you requested, to improve our services and/or to make you aware of other services that we deliver. We will only share with or disclose this data to Third Parties that support our services, such as credit card companies, payment gateway providers and parties that help us analyze the information we collect so that we can administer, support, improve and develop our business and services to you. In addition, we may need to provide your information to any distribution partners or affiliates so that they can support us in providing the service you requested. By submitting your personal data, you agree to this transfer to, storing and/or processing by Third Parties as well.

You agree that we may allow carefully selected third parties, including marketing and advertising companies, our affiliates and associates, to contact you occasionally about services that may be of interest to you. They may contact you by telephone, SMS as well as by e-mail. If you change your mind about being contacted by these companies in the future, please let us know by using the contact details set out below and/or by amending your profile accordingly. If our business enters a joint venture with, purchases or is sold to or merged with another business entity, your information may be disclosed or transferred to the target company, our new business partners or owners or their advisors.

We may use the information that you provide to us if we are under a duty to disclose or share your information in order to comply with (and/or where we believe we are under a duty to comply with) any legal obligation, or in order to enforce our Website Terms and any other agreement, or to protect the rights of AUTODATA, its owners, affiliates and/or partners. The laws governing data protection in these countries or jurisdictions may differ from the laws in the UAE. By your voluntary submission of information to us, you are consenting to the disclosure, transfer and use of your personal information outside of the UAE for the purposes described in this privacy policy.

Security and data retention

We take steps to protect your information from unauthorized access and against unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction and damage. We will keep your information for a reasonable period or as long as the law requires.
